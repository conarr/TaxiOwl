One App, Many Features, Many Benefits

See what TaxiOwl can do for you...

Our route plotting system is simple to use with minimum effort required to plan your journey.

Your location and destination can be typed in or, for your convenience, simply pinned on the interactive map.

Once plotted the app will calculate the optimum route factoring in traffic, time and diversions.

When the taxi strays too far from the plotted optimum route an alert will display and sound.

Routes can be saved for reuse including journey, taxi firm, distance and cost.

Access a database of pre-rated taxi firms for the city or region you are in.

Contribute to our database of taxi firms by rating the taxi firm you just used.

Be safe in an unrated taxi. Instant emergency call and SMS if you feel under threat.
